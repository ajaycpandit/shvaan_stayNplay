About Shvaan Pet Care

Small by design. Personal by nature. We believe every dog deserves to feel truly at home.

Why we started Shvaan

Shvaan Pet Care was born from a simple belief: dogs are family, and they deserve to be treated like it when their people are away. After years of caring for dogs of all breeds and temperaments, we built the kind of boarding experience we'd want for our own pets.

We keep our guest numbers intentionally small — not because we have to, but because we choose to. Smaller means calmer, safer, and more personal. Every dog gets one-on-one time, a consistent routine, and a warm space to truly relax.

Our space

Our boarding space is clean, comfortable, and designed with dogs in mind — indoor rest areas, a secure outdoor play area, and enough room for every guest to have their own comfortable spot. We follow a structured daily routine with feeding, play, rest, and walks.

We enforce strict vaccination requirements for all guests (Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella) and keep careful records of each dog's health, diet, and medications. Safety isn't an afterthought here — it's the foundation.

Our Promise

What you can always count on

🛡️ Safety first

Every guest is screened at a meet & greet before their first stay. Our space is fully secured, and dogs are supervised at all times — no unsupervised mixing of dogs who haven't been introduced.

❤️ Genuine attention

We limit the number of guests at any time so every dog gets real, individual care — not just a bed in a crowded room. Your dog will know they're loved.

📸 Stay in the loop

We send photo updates throughout every stay so you can see your pup thriving. No news isn't always good news — we believe in keeping you connected.

🎓 Experience you can trust

Years of hands-on experience with dogs of all sizes, breeds, and personalities — including seniors, anxious dogs, and those needing medication management.
