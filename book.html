🔒 Reservations are made through the client portal.
Log in to your account to request boarding or day care. If you don't have a dog profile set up yet, you'll need to add your dog first before submitting a reservation request — this ensures we have everything we need to take great care of your pup.
Log In to Client Portal
Once logged in: go to Dogs to add or check your dog's profile, then visit Reservations to submit a request.
Why do I need a dog profile first? Your dog's profile stores their breed, vaccination records, feeding routine, and any special care notes. This means every member of our team knows exactly how to care for your dog before they even arrive — and we can calculate accurate pricing for your stay.
