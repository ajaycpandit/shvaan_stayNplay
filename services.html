Good to Know

What to expect

💉 Vaccinations required

All guests must be current on Rabies, DHPP, and Bordetella. Please bring or send vaccination records before your dog's first stay. No exceptions — it keeps every guest safe.

🍖 Food & feeding

We strongly recommend bringing your dog's own food to avoid any stomach upset from a diet change. Just pack it in clearly labelled bags with feeding instructions and we'll follow your routine exactly.

🧸 Comforts from home

You're welcome to bring a favourite bed, blanket, or toy — familiar scents can make a real difference, especially for first-time boarders.

🕑 Drop-off & pick-up

Drop-off from 7:00 am, pick-up by 7:00 pm daily. Check-in and check-out times are by appointment — please don't arrive without confirming first so we can give your dog a proper welcome.

💊 Medications

We're happy to administer medications at no extra charge. Please provide clear written instructions, the medication in its original packaging, and any special notes about your dog's routine.

📋 Cancellations

We ask for at least 48 hours' notice for cancellations. Cancellations with less notice may forfeit a deposit if one was taken. We understand life happens — just let us know as soon as you can.
