Got Questions?

We've got answers

Everything you need to know before your dog's first stay.

We require proof of current Rabies, Distemper-Parvo (DHPP), and Bordetella (kennel cough) vaccinations. All vaccines must be administered by a licensed veterinarian and be current at the time of your dog's stay. Please bring a copy of your vet records or have them emailed to us in advance.

Yes, with accommodations. Intact males are managed separately during group play. Female dogs in heat are accepted but stay in a private suite with individual care and are not included in group play sessions for the safety of all guests.
Drop-off: Monday–Sunday, 7:00 AM – 11:00 AM. Standard checkout is by 10:00 AM. Pickups after 12:00 PM (noon) will incur a half-day late pick-up fee based on your dog's size. After-hours pickup (after 6:00 PM) is available by appointment for an additional $15 fee.
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover), Venmo, Zelle, and cash. A 50% deposit is required at booking to secure your spot, especially during peak holidays (Thanksgiving, Christmas/New Year, Memorial Day, Fourth of July, Labor Day). The balance is due on or before check-in.
Cancellations made 5 or more days before the stay receive a full refund of the deposit. Cancellations within 3–4 days receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 48 hours or no-shows forfeit the deposit. Holiday bookings (defined above) require 7-day notice for a full refund.
We have a partnering 24-hour emergency vet clinic nearby and will contact you and your listed emergency contact immediately. All guests require an emergency medical authorization form signed at check-in. Any vet expenses are the owner's responsibility and we will assist with coordination and transport.
